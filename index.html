The Reign of Baby Names in the XXI Century

By Rafael Oliveira

Since the turn of the century, millions of babies have been named across the U.S., but some names stand out for their lasting popularity. By exploring a baby name data, from 1999 to 2023, we uncovered which names have truly defined a generation.

One clear standout among boys' names is Jacob. As the chart shows, since 1999, more than half a million boys have been given this name. Jacob dominated the early 2000s, reflecting a broader trend toward strong, traditional names that resonated with many American families.


Among girls, Emma proved to be just as iconic. More than 450,000 girls were named Emily during the same period, making it one of the most enduring and beloved choices for baby girls in the early 21st century.
